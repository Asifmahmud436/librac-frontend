Librac University

LIBRAC University (LIBRACU) is a private university in Dhaka, Bangladesh, established in 2024. It is known for its commitment to quality education, innovation, and social impact. The university offers undergraduate, graduate, and postgraduate programs in various disciplines, including business, law, social sciences, computer science, engineering, and public health. It follows a liberal arts education model, encouraging students to engage in interdisciplinary studies and develop critical thinking skills.

Our Departments

CSE

The CSE (Computer Science and Engineering) Department at LIBRAC University is one of the prominent departments, focusing on producing skilled graduates in areas such as software development, data science, AI, cybersecurity, and more.

EEE

The EEE (Electrical and Electronic Engineering) department at LIBRAC University is similarly regarded as a reputable and competitive program in Bangladesh.It emphasizes a balance of knowledge, preparing students for careers in both academia and the industry.

BBA

The BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) program at LIBRAC University is widely considered one of the top business programs in Bangladesh, attracting students who are interested in both business theory and practical management skills.

ECO

The Economics (ECO) department at LIBRAC University is recognized for its strong academic foundation. It is a popular choice for students interested in policy-making, research, and understanding economic trends and challenges, both in Bangladesh and globally.
